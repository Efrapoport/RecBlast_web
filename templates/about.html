About Us

RecBlast was brought to you by an enthusiastic team at Tel-Aviv University which truly believes that cloud computing is the future of computational biology.
In order to promote scientific discovery, we set out to improve the accessibility to performing complicated tasks such as multiple bi-directional and reciprocal BLAST alignments.
RecBlasts' code is open source, and we welcome contributions: https://github.com/neuhofmo/RecBlast
